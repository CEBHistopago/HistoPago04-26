# **App Name**: CreditFlow

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure user registration and login with role-based access control for vendors and customers.
- Credit Sale Recording: Record credit sales with details like customer, amount, items, and due date.
- Payment Application: Apply customer payments to outstanding credit balances, tracking payment dates and amounts.
- Compliance Reporting Tool: Flag and report customer defaults, sending reports to the database or other services when appropriate.
- Credit Information Verification: Use customer identification number to check their credit information to approve new credit requests. A tool will evaluate credit data, possibly using external services or APIs, and make recommendations or flag issues.
- Sales reporting and Analytics: Analyze and present reports on different key sales performance indicators to different parties according to their permission level

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#293B5F) to inspire confidence.
- Background color: Light gray (#E2E8F0) to ensure legibility of page content.
- Accent color: Teal (#3D5A80) for key interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif suitable for both headlines and body text, which will lend the app a modern, neutral look.
- Use minimalist, professional icons for navigation and actions.
- A clean, intuitive layout with clear hierarchy for ease of use.
- Subtle transitions and animations for feedback and enhanced user experience.
